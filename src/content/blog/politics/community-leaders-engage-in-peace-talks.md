Community leaders, youth representatives, and security officials met today at the Atiba Town Hall to foster dialogue regarding peace and security in Oyo.

The meeting, which lasted for four hours, focused on preventative measures to ensure a safe environment for all residents during the upcoming festivals.

"Peace is the bedrock of development," said Chief Alabi. "We must work together to secure our future."
