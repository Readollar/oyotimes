The much-anticipated Oyo Youth Football League began yesterday at the Durbar Stadium with a thrilling match between *Oyo Mesi FC* and *Atiba United*.

The match ended in a 2-1 victory for Oyo Mesi FC, setting the tone for what promises to be an electrifying season. Scouts from major Nigerian clubs were spotted in the stands, looking for the next big talent.

"This league is about more than just football; it's about keeping our youth engaged and productive," said the League Chairman.
