In a bid to improve the learning environment for students, the state government has officially commissioned blocks of new classrooms at Olivet Baptist High School and Ladigbolu Grammar School.

The project includes modern furniture, whiteboards, and improved ventilation systems. Students expressed their joy at the new developments, stating it would motivate them to study harder.

The Commissioner for Education reiterated the government's commitment to quality education for every child in Oyo State.
